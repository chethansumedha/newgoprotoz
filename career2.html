Senior UI/UX Designer

Vacancies

2

Experience

3-6 years
(*Portfolio matters)

Location

Bangalore

Working Week

5 Days

  • Job Description
  • We need a team player who can turn a variety of requirements into clear and simple solutions. If you are driven by a passion for great UI UX design to deliver solutions that are creatively inspired and pragmatically realized, we need you.

    • Responsibilities and Duties:

  • Gathering and evaluating user requirements, in collaboration with product managers and engineers.
  • Illustrating design ideas using storyboards, process flows and sitemaps.
  • Designing graphics user interface elements, like menus, tabs and widgets.
  • Develop UI mockups and prototypes that clearly illustrate how sites function and look like.
  • Create original graphics designs (e.g. images, sketches and tables).

    • Experience/Qualifications:

  • 3-8 years of experience in creating visuals across web & mobile.
  • Expertise in design software like Adobe Photoshop, Sketch and Illustrator.
  • Knowledge of wireframe tools (e.g. Wireframe.cc and InVision).
  • Team spirit; strong communication skills to collaborate with various stakeholders.
  • Degree in Visual Arts, BFA etc will be a bonus.
  • Have a great taste in visual design (backed by a good portfolio).
  • Ability to create visuals for mobile (ios & android), web apps & responsive websites.
  • Proficient with a range of design tools; an expert in at least 2 design tools (Sketch preferred).
  • Expert on exporting assets & designing UI based on platform standards.
  • Ability to juggle between multiple projects.
  • Excellent on typography, alignments, balance & motion.
